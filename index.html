Digital Audiometer

the 1st fully digital and entirely computer-based
runs on Windows, Mac, Sun, Unix and Linux

Digital Audiometer is a computer software application for investigating hearing thresholds in a 0 Hz to 22,000 Hz frequency range and -20 dB to 100 dB SPL range. It runs on popular desktop hardware, including portable computers, and does not require any external or internal proprietary devices. It is very precise, easy to use, and due to the stability of digital circuitry, needs to be calibrated only once.

.

Digital Audiometer allows for a very accurate evaluation of hearing loss, auditory problems and capabilities.


Digital Audiometer Applications
• Ideal for those who perform standard hearing tests in clinics, schools or the workplace.
• Great value to audiologists, nurses and medical professionals such as ENT and family doctors.
• Its parameters make it an ideal instrument for research and teaching in the fields of acoustics, psychoacoustics, audio, electroacoustics, medicine, audiology and speech pathology.
• Very useful in fitting today's more advanced digital and semi-digital hearing aids.

  Features
  • Fully digital and entirely computer-based (any platform)
  • Does not use any external or internal proprietary devices
  • Fast, reliable and easy to use
  • Portable, if installed on a portable computer
  • Allows for all standard audiometric tests
  • Offers a choice of automatic
  • Stores all audio signals in digital format
  • Offers high precision and consistency of measurements
  • Offers high quality and stability of the test signals

  Parameters
Test signal type Pure Tones and Warble Tones
Frequency range 250 Hz to 4000 Hz
Frequency resolution 1Hz
S/N 96 dB (in 16 bit systems)
Amplitude (SPL) range -20 dB to 100 dB
Amplitude resolution 1dB
